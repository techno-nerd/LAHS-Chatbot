Head Coach: Seth Tasman
Coach Tasman currently serves as the Los Altos boys water polo and swimming head coach. Coach Tasman has successfully been coaching water polo at the youth, high school, and college level for the past 10 years. In 2019 High School season his Los Altos team went undefeated in the SCVAL De Anza Division and earned the 5 seed in the CCS Open Division, where they lost to St. Francis by one goal. Finishing the season with a record of 24 - 3. His Los Altos team won the SCVAL De Anza Championship in 2019 and 2017, as well as qualified for the CCS Open Division. both years. In 2018 they made it to the championship game of the Divisions 1 CCS Finals.
Coach Tasman also runs NorCal Aquatics Water Polo Club which is one of the top club teams in the Bay Area.
Coach Tasman was a two-sport athlete at Iona College (NY). He captained both the swimming and water polo team during his tenure. He finished his senior season top 10 in the NCAA in scoring and had the 4th highest goals per game average. For swimming, he set 7 school records and was an all-conference swimmer.
“I coach because I love competition and winning, but not more than I love to see young players develop into well-rounded adults.”
