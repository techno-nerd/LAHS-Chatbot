California Scholarship Federation
What is CSF?
CSF stands for California Scholarship Federation. It is an academic honors society for active and academically minded students. CSF is not an active club.
How do you join?
At the beginning of every semester, students will use the previous semesters grades/report card for submission.
Students are admitted into CSF solely based on their semester grades.
Application Fall 2024
Who is eligible to apply?
Freshman, sophomores, juniors, and seniors.
Freshmen can’t apply for membership until the start of the second semester.
How do students pay my semester dues?
The semester dues can be paid in person at the finance office or via the
school webstore
. Students requiring financial assistance should notify Señora Salsman de Rodriguex.  Dues are $5.00.
How do I become a Gold Seal Bearer or Life Member?
Students who have a minimum of 4 semesters of membership (including one from their senior year) qualify for Gold Seal status. Students are notified by the CSF adviser in May.
Questions?
Email Señora Salsman de Rodriguex at terri.salsmanderodriguex@mvla.net. She can also be found in Room 911.
