LAHS Tutorial Center
English
Our mission is to provide the highest quality free tutoring, driven by an equity lens, aimed at supporting the success of LAHS students, staff, and community.
How can we help?
Please visit us at the Tutorial Center! This space is for you! You are welcome to visit us if you would like to work with a tutor, need a space to study or complete assignments, and if you just need a space to hang out. You are always welcome here. Tutors can help you prepare for upcoming exams, brainstorm + strategize studying techniques, discuss + break down course concepts & problem sets, organization support and much more!

How to Prepare for a Tutoring Session
Bring all necessary course materials. For example: computer, books, notes, assignments, rubrics, syllabi etc.
If there are specific questions that you need assistance with, please bring them with you
Arrive on time to meet with your tutor
Ensure that you sign-in to the Tutorial Center upon arrival (there is a Chromebook at the entrance and QR Codes on the tables)

Brainfuse
Brainfuse HelpNow, is a live, online tutoring service for students from Kindergarten to 12th grade that also provides writing analysis, skill-building resources and collaboration tools. Completely FREE for students.
Provided through the California State Library, HelpNow helps tutors and students communicate in real time, enables writing assignments to be expertly evaluated any time of day and offers a broad selection of practice tests for all levels in a number of subjects.
It can be found on the
Children & Teen Learning Resources page
, the
Career & Skill-Building page
and also at
MountainView.gov/BrainfuseHelpNow
. A Library card is not required to access the resource.

Brainfuse
Brainfuse HelpNow es un servicio de tutoría en línea en vivo para estudiantes desde el jardín de infantes hasta el grado 12 que también proporciona análisis de escritura, recursos para el desarrollo de habilidades y herramientas de colaboración. Es completamente GRATIS para estudiantes.
Proporcionado a través de la Biblioteca del Estado de California, HelpNow ayuda a los tutores y estudiantes a comunicarse en tiempo real, permite que las tareas de escritura sean evaluadas por expertos en cualquier momento del día y ofrece una amplia selección de pruebas de práctica para todos los niveles en una serie de materias.
Se puede encontrar en la página de
Recursos de aprendizaje para niños y adolescentes
, la
página de Desarrollo de habilidades
y carreras y también en MountainView.gov/BrainfuseHelpNow. No se requiere una tarjeta de biblioteca para acceder al recurso.

Peer Tutors (Volunteer or For Credit)
Hello Los Altos High School Students! We need you! We are searching for additional Peer Tutors for the 2023-2024 school year in all subject areas. This is a great opportunity to get involved in your community and help students! It is also a great opportunity to learn new skills, receive credit or volunteer hours, and gain experience for your resume!
If you are interested in getting involved as a Peer Tutor or have any questions, please reach out to Ms. Paredes via email at
