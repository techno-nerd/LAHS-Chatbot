Varsity Head Coach: Mary Donahue
Coach Donahue has been coaching for 17 years. Mary played Field Hockey at Stanford University. After college, she umpired for 10 years at the high school, club and college levels and coached part-time at Stanford. She has been the head coach at Los Altos HS since 2007 and the director of San Jose Fly Field Hockey Club since 2008. She coaches because she love kids and loves sports.She loves how sports can enrich our kids' lives in so many ways. Mary has been honored as Coach of the year for Los Altos in 2013-2014 and in 2019-2020. She has also been named CCS Honor Coach in 2016-2017 and 2022-23.
