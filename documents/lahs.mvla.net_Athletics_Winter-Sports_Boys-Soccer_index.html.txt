Head varsity Coach Roberto Magellan
Head varsity Coach Roberto Magellan
Coach Magallen, has worked at Los Altos High since 2019. He grew up in Brazil playing Futsal, Beach Soccer, and Soccer. He played youth soccer for Club Flamengo of Rio de Janeiro and for the University of San Francisco Soccer team on an athletic scholarship. He has a Physical Education and Psychology Degrees. He has played for the US National Futsal Team. Coached 14 years at De Anza Force, and now at Palo Alto Soccer Club. Daughter Stephanie attended Cal Poly and played for the Brazil U20 National Team. Son Christian is attending UCLA. He coaches for the love the game.
