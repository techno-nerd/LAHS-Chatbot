Current LAHS Students
Please fill out the
Transcript Request Form
for current students and email the form to the registrar at
elena.baquero@mvla.net
.
Transcripts are free for current LAHS students.
Make sure you have updated your Naviance College list.
.................................................................................................................
Former LAHS Students (Alumni)
Alumni and are entitled to two free transcripts
. Additional transcripts cost $5/each.
1. Fill out the
Alumni Transcript Request Form
Alumni transcripts are ONLY sent via US Mail.
2. Provide a copy of your current picture ID
3. Pay the $5 processing fee
only if you've already received your two free transcripts
Pay online via our
webstore
. Once in the Webstore, select LAHS School Store in the top left corner, then search for
Alumni Transcripts
OR
Mail a check or money order
payable to "Los Altos High School"
4.
Email the
Transcript Request Form
to the Registrar
(elena.baquero@mvla.net)
OR
Mail your request to:
Los Altos High School
Attn: Registrar
201 Almond Ave.
Los Altos, CA  94022
