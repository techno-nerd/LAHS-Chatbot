Los Altos High School 2024-2025
About Rally Class/Sideline Cheer:
- You will cheer for football and basketball season and rallies.
- You will participate in all group fundraisers.
SIDELINE CHEER TRYOUT REQUIREMENTS:
Attend all scheduled workshops (excused if currently participating in a school sport) There will be a 2nd tryout date for those affected. Dates TBA.
JV must perform the band chant, 1 sideline dance and the cheer learned at the workshop: Must attend stunt evaluation day, May 1st. STUNTS: Must perform a 2 foot prep. Can be any stunt position other than a front spot.
Varsity must perform the band chant, 1 sideline dance, and the cheer learned at the workshop, as well as an original routine.  Music will be supplied.  Must attend the stunt evaluation day, May 1st. STUNTS: Must perform an extension cupie and an extension lib. Can be any stunt position other than a front spot.
