Assistant Coach: Matthew Saul
Matt began Coaching at LAHS in February 2018. He has been Coaching overall for 20 years. He began coaching Little League T-Ball in 1999, with his son. Coached through majors with him. Matt then started coaching at MVLAGS with his daughter from 6U through her starting high school.
He currently works in the  IT field at Campbell Union HS District for the last 24 years and enjoys that. He also enjoys an occasional game of golf and time with his family.
He enjoys coaching because he likes watching the players at all levels grow and get better. "It's fun to be out there helping and watching the game!"
