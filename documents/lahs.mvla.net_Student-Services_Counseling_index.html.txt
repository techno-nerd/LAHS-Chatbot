Office hours & Contacts
Office hours & Contacts
Counselors welcome their students to attend their office hours during student brunch and lunchtime on a drop-in basis. If there are are other students waiting, visits will be limited to 10 minutes. Arrangements for additional time can be made if needed.
Counselor office hours are only available to students.
Parents should email or call their student's counselor as needed to determine next steps.
