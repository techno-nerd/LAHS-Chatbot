Commencement Ceremony
Commencement Ceremony
Friday, June 6, 2025       6:00pm
LAHS Football Field
The commencement ceremony will begin promptly at 6pm and last approximately 90 minutes.
Everyone is welcome. No tickets are required to enter.
Seating is on a first-come, first-served basis. The gates open at 5pm. Entrance to the field will be through the parking lot.
Yearbook
Yearbook
Created by students for students, the LAHS Yearbook highlights the people, stories, and events that make LAHS unique.
Yearbooks are available for sale online and at the finance office for $100. The price increase to $110 on 2/1.
Yearbooks sell out, so we recommend ordering early.
100% Class Participation at all Events
100% Class Participation at all Events
The school and PTSA’s goal is for 100% of the senior class to attend all the senior events.
Financial support is available for prom and picnic. Students should go to the finance office to request a fee waiver. Financial assistance for grad night can be requested via the online sign-up link.
Prom and picnic tickets can also be paid in installments at the finance office throughout the year.
