Welcome to the LAHS Student Community Leaders (SCL)
The Student Community Leaders (SCL) program is dedicated to building a school environment that embodies inclusion, celebrates diversity, and promotes wellness for all students. By organizing meaningful service learning and wellness-focused activities, SCL plays a crucial role in fostering a supportive, thriving community at Los Altos High School. This leadership team empowers students to make a positive impact on their peers and develop essential skills for personal and professional growth.
Mission Statement:
SCL's focus is to build a community at Los Altos High School that embodies inclusion by celebrating diversity and advocating well-being of the student body and staff. SCL strives to make this mission more accessible through a variety of activities tailored to the diverse student population throughout the school year.
What We Do:
Leadership Development:
SCL provides students with opportunities to enhance their leadership and public speaking skills. Members work closely with school administration to address the needs of the student body and implement impactful initiatives.
Wellness Initiatives:
The SCL team organizes events and projects aimed at promoting mental health, physical wellness, and overall student well-being. From stress relief activities to Wellness Week programming, these efforts ensure students feel supported throughout the school year.
Service Learning:
SCL plans and facilitates service learning projects for both the team and the wider student body. These initiatives encourage students to engage in meaningful acts of service, fostering a culture of giving back and community engagement.
Community Building:
By hosting activities that bring students together, SCL strengthens connections across campus and ensures that every student feels a sense of belonging at Los Altos High School.
How to Get Involved:
Join SCL:
Students interested in becoming part of the SCL team are encouraged to apply during the spring semester, coinciding with the course selection window. Keep an eye out for announcements with application details and deadlines.
Attend SCL-Sponsored Events:
Participate in service drives, wellness activities, and other events hosted by SCL throughout the school year. These events are open to all students and provide great opportunities to connect with peers and contribute to the school community. Check the Student Activities Calendar for upcoming events!
Share Your Ideas:
SCL values student input and is always open to suggestions for new activities or ways to improve wellness and inclusion at LAHS. Feel free to reach out to any SCL member or share feedback using the available suggestion forms.
