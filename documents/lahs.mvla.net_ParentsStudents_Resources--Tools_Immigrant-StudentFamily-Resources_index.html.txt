Immigrant Student/Family Resources
Mountain View-Los Altos High School District is committed to ensuring a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for all students, regardless of immigration status. This page provides important information on student rights, safety at school, and community resources for students, families, and staff.
At Mountain View-Los Altos High School District, every student has the right to feel safe and supported at school. Our district follows state and federal laws that protect all students, including those who are undocumented or from immigrant families.
