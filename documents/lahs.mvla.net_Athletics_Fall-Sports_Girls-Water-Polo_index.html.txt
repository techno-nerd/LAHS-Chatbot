About Coach Tasman
About Coach Tasman
Head Coach: Katie Tasman
Coach Katie Tasman currently serves as the Head Coach of the Los Altos girls' water polo and Swimming team. She has been Coaching for over 9 years. During the 2017 and 2018 high school seasons, she led the Eagles to back-to-back D1 CCS Championships. In 2015 she led her team to be SCVAL De Anza Champions. Coach Katie played collegiate water polo at San Jose State University from 2009-2012 under legendary Coach Lou Tully. While at SJSU, Coach Tasman received multiple best defensive player awards, as well as most improved. In her junior year SJSU finished 5th in the nation and 4th in the Mountain Pacific league.
Coach Tasman also runs NorCal Aquatics Water Polo Club which is one of the top clubs in the bay area.
During high school, Coach Tasman was a four-time MVP on San Benito's varsity team water polo team, averaging over 100 goals in her junior and senior years. As their captain, Coach Tasman lead San Benito to their first league championship, leading her to receive first-team all-CCS honors. Coach Tasman brings a wealth of knowledge - and contagious enthusiasm - to coaching all levels of boys and girls
“I coach to help athletes find joy in athletics, and fall in love with something I hold near and dear to my. heart.”
