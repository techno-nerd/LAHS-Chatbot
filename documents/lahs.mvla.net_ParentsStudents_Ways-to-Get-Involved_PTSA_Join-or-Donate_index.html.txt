JOIN OR DONATE
The PTSA raises money through both Memberships and Direct Ask donations.  We rely mostly on Direct Ask donations which cover expenses and campus programs such as STEAM week, Writers Week, Teacher Training, Library services, and Staff Appreciation events.  For a full list of what your donation will help support, go to
What We Fund
.
Thank you for continuing to support LAHS and parent-led organizations during these unprecedented times. Donations of money and time will help us to continue to enhance the educational experience for each student at Los Altos High.
Memberships
:
A basic membership ($20) comes with one login for the online student directory and a family membership ($40) comes with two log-ins.
Direct Ask Donation
:
A Direct Ask donation of $200 per student is recommended, but any amount is appreciated!
And corporate matching donations are welcomed.
